EFFICIENCY

The local public transport service provides urban and suburban transport services in Turin and in other 26 neighbouring towns.
The system is used by 200 million passengers a year, with 44.26 million km operated each year by 1,215 buses and 240 trams.

ECO-FRIENDLY

The ecologic management of the city public transport("Green Public Procurement") has been made mandatory from the recent legal news (legge n. 221, del 28 dicembre 2015).
It is the insertion of evaluation criterial about the ambient quality in every ordinary procedure concerning the Public Administration.
